# Product Context - Valeris Coaching Website

## Why This Product Exists

Swiss enterprise buyers systematically verify web presence before engaging advisors. A domain parked page or absence signals lack of seriousness. The website exists to convert "Lucas Challamel was recommended to me" into "I should take the call."

## User Personas

### Primary: Swiss CTO / VP Engineering

- 40-55 years old, based in Romandie or Swiss-German region
- Managing 20-200 engineers at a CHF 50-500M company
- Facing: AI governance pressure from board, team delivery challenges, or personal leadership transition
- Behaviour: receives recommendation, Googles "Valeris Coaching" or "Lucas Challamel consulting", spends 3-8 minutes on site
- Decision: "Is this person credible? Should I take the call?"

### Secondary: HR / Talent Leader

- Looking for executive coaching for a technical leader
- Needs to justify the engagement to procurement
- Wants: credentials, methodology, price indication

### Tertiary: Compliance Officer / CISO

- Evaluating AI governance advisory
- Needs: ISO 42001 expertise, framework knowledge, Swiss regulatory awareness
- Trust signals: certifications, standards references, Swiss presence

## Value Proposition

> Valeris Coaching helps Swiss enterprises succeed at technology transformation by aligning leadership capability, team execution, and governance frameworks - delivered by someone who has built, led, and governed at scale.

## Three Service Offerings

| # | Offering | FR Name | Buyer | Entry Point |
|---|---------|---------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | AI Activation & Governance | Activation IA & Gouvernance | CTO, CISO, Compliance | CHF 8,500 diagnostic |
| 2 | Engineering Team Performance | Performance des Equipes d'Ingenierie | CTO, VP Engineering | CHF 9,000 health check |
| 3 | Technical Executive Coaching | Coaching de Dirigeants Techniques | The executive, HR | CHF 1,500 clarity session |

## Competitive Position

Upper-right quadrant: high technical depth + advisory/coaching delivery model. Sparsely populated in Romandie.

## Business Rules

- Never use "we" language (solo practice)
- Never offer free discovery sessions (paid diagnostic, credit against full engagement)
- Price ranges visible on service pages (signals confidence, filters leads)
- CTA is always "Book a conversation", never "Contact us"
- No client logos without explicit permission
- Trilingual: French (Romandie primary market), German (Swiss-German reach), English (international)
