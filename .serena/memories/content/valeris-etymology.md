# Valeris - Name Etymology

Latin verb **valeo**: to be well, to be healthy, to be strong.
**Valeris** = 2nd person future tense: "you will be strong", "you will be well".

Philosophy: with the right support, the right decisions and the right mindset, everything will be fine.
Guides every engagement: strengthen leaders, teams and organisations so they become durably capable, autonomous and confident.

Used on the About page in all 4 languages as the opening section with the flower+leaves logo.
