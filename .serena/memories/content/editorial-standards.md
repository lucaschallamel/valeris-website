# Editorial Standards

## No Dashes
Client dislikes dashes (tirets) as separators. All 33 occurrences removed.
Replace with: commas, semicolons, colons, full stops, or parentheses.

## Context Engineer (not Systems Engineer)
Coaching diagram: Technical Lead -> Context Engineer
Aligns with "Code is Cattle, Context is King" philosophy.
Context Engineer = designs the contexts in which AI agents deliver value.

## Methodology Steps (EN)
1. Diagnostic (same)
2. Scoping (not "Framing")
3. Delivery & Transfer (not "Support")

## Hero Headline
FR: "Un leadership technologique qui fait la difference." (not "qui livre")
EN: "Technology leadership that delivers." (kept)

## Languages
- FR: all accents mandatory, "Livre blanc" not "Whitepaper"
- DE: UTF-8 umlauts (not ASCII ae/oe/ue), "Mitarbeitende" not "Mitarbeiter"
- EN: British spelling, no contractions (formal tone), "French-speaking Switzerland"
- IT: "leadership tecnologica" (feminine), "Modalita di collaborazione", "Per iniziare"
